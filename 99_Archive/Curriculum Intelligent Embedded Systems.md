# Curriculum: Intelligent Embedded Systems (LM-32)
**University of Bologna** | **Academic Year:** 2025-26 | **Campus:** Cesena

This curriculum offers two tracks:
- **Local Track:** Entirely at UniBo Cesena.
- **EIT Digital Track:** 1st year at UniBo, 2nd year at a partner university abroad.

## Final Examination (30 CFU)
The final phase consists of the Master's Thesis, which can be prepared:
- Locally at UniBo.
- Abroad via Erasmus+ Traineeship.
- In partnership with a company (Internship for Final Examination).
